# Claim Status Instruction

All claims in the living research book must have a status.

No claim may be used in book prose unless its status allows it.

## Allowed statuses

Use only these statuses:

- candidate
- needs_review
- supported
- weakly_supported
- contradicted
- rejected
- promoted_to_chapter

## Status definitions

### candidate

A possible claim extracted from source material.

It has not been reviewed.

Do not use in chapter prose.

### needs_review

A potentially useful claim that is unclear, weakly sourced, ambiguous, too broad, privacy-sensitive, or possibly contradictory.

Do not use in chapter prose until reviewed.

### supported

A claim with adequate source support.

May be used in chapter prose.

### weakly_supported

A plausible claim with limited support.

May be used only if clearly caveated.

Example caveats:

- “Current evidence suggests...”
- “A weak signal is emerging that...”
- “Based on the available sources, this appears to be...”
- “This should be treated as an early observation, not a settled conclusion.”

### contradicted

A claim that conflicts with credible evidence.

Do not use as a factual statement.

May be discussed only as a contradiction or contested point.

### rejected

A claim that is unsupported, noisy, irrelevant, unsafe, duplicative, misleading, or too weak.

Do not use.

### promoted_to_chapter

A claim approved by the Editor for use in book prose.

May be used by the Author.

## Required claim fields

Each claim should include:

- claim ID
- claim text
- claim type
- status
- source IDs
- evidence strength
- source quality
- first seen
- last seen
- related entities
- contradiction status
- editor note
- publication decision

## Claim types

Use these claim types:

- definition
- observation
- trend
- technical pattern
- risk
- limitation
- example
- contradiction
- interpretation
- open question

## Author usage rule

The Author may use only:

- supported
- weakly_supported, with caveat
- promoted_to_chapter

The Author must not use:

- candidate
- needs_review
- contradicted
- rejected

## Editor rule

The Editor must review all claims before promotion.

## Final rule

No source ID, no claim.
No claim, no chapter fact.
